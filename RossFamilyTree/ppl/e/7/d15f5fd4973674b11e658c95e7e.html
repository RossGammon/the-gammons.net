Narrative

Margaret died of T.B. in Sanitorium in North Carolina. Margaret lost both her husband and twin sons in successive years of 1914,1915 and 1916. Family story says that when a family relation went to the funeral of Louis, the first twin, that died, the surviving twin, Fred, stated that he would also die within one year. The perdiction came true.
