Narrative

Elizabeth Dunsford (Cutcliffe) and her husband William were married in England in 1836 and came to Prince Edward Island, Canada with Richard Cutcliffe, Elixabeth's father. Elizabeth Left PEI and returned to England. A Memorial Stone to Elizabeth is in Crapaud Peoples Cemetery, Prince Edward Island, Canada, Lot 29-4.
