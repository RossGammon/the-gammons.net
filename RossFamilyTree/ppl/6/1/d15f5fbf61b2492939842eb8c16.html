Narrative

 

Adrian Cutcliffe, recorded in the Visitation of 1620, as the third son, and as then of the age of 30 yrs., and unmarried. He married Istly, at Ilfracombe, 1630, Joan Hammett, who was buried at Ilfracombe, 1642, Joane Martnoll, who survived her husband, and was buried at Ilfracombe 1681, having had issue a daughter. Adrian Cutcliffe ws buried 1646."
