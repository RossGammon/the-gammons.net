Narrative

At her marriage with Alexander Mathie (14/1/1925), Elsa Ruby GOODGAME states that she was widowed on 25th November 1924, and that she has one living child (my grandmother Doreen), and two dead children. She also states that she was born in Kent, England, and that her father is Willam GOODGAME (Carter), and her mother is Mary Ann GOODGAME (nee DITSDALE?). This may be a lie, or because her mother died young, she was just stating all she knew, and these people may have been her carers.
