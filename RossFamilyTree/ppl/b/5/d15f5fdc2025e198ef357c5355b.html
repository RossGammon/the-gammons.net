"The Rev. Robert Cutcliffe, who was for fifty years Vicar of Beer Seaton, co. Devon. He married Elizabeth Grainger of Exeter, and died without surviving issue 1838, aged 77 years, leaving his wife, who died 1852, aged 88 years. They were both buried in Seaton Church.
The Rev. Robert Cutcliffe was appointed Vicar in 1791. Matriculated age 18, Exter College, Oxford March 1, 1779.
