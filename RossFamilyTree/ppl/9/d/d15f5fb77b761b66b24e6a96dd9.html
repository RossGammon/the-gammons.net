Narrative

Home: 3 Mar 1908 - 17 Coley Hill, Reading, Berkshire, England (birth).
1953 - 1957: 29A Queens Road, Parkstone, Dorset, England (BT Directories Ref: 138).
Auntie Mary lived at 29A Queens Road & nursed her parent until they died. She sold up and bought the little home (bungalow she called it) 10 Birds Hill Road, Poole. Francis visited her there in 1982 (Francis email 2/11/05).
I visited her (probably Birds Hill Road) in 1994 in a hire car.
When I visited her again by train in 1996, she was in a nursing home in Parkstone.
