Narrative

Original marriage 1540 to Katherine, daughter of Thomas Hext. Katherine died in 1563. In 1564 married to Joane Reynell and had one child Susan bapt. 1565.
Source for birth date,place of birth, names of parents, date and place of marriage and name of wife. One record states the they were married in Kingston and the other states that they were married in Braunton.
Date of birth source
"John Cutcliffe, who married, at Georgeham, 30th August 1540 for his first wife, Katherine, daughter of Thomas Hext, by Jane, daughter of John Fortescue, by whom he had issue eleven childeren. After the death of Katherine (Hext) who was buried at Georgeham, 4 Nov 1563, John Cutcliffe married secondly, at Georgeham, 29 May 1564, Joane Reynell, by whom he had issue a daughter."
