Narrative

1841 aged 11 Chaddlewood Fm, PStM, as agric. labourer working for Aaron Adams.
1851 aged 23 82 Bridge Tenements PStM with wife Emilene as agric lab (called Richard?)
1861 18 Dark St Lane with wife Emilene (Emlin), sons Edwin, William, Michal, daughters Elizabeth & Emlin - spelling doubtful on census form
1871 aged 43 at 7 Sidney Cotts PStM with wife Emilin, sons Edwin, Frederick, Albert & daus Emlin , Anna
After the death of his first wife in 1872, Michael appears in
1881 at 7 Sydney Cotts. PStM, aged 54 with Mary Jane, wife, aged 36 yrs, b. Ilfordcombe, with Annie,daughter,14 yrs, b. Plympton St Marys, and Albert, son, 10 yrs, b. Plympton plus 2 stepsons (presumably Mary Janes's from her 1st marriage) called John Bowden, 8 & Thomas, 6 both born in PStM.
1891 Michael is 63 yrs, general labourer, with Mary Jane, Frederick son, 9 yrs, George, son, 8 yrs, Henry son, 6 yrs, Lily,daughter,4 yrs, all born Plympton. Then living at Brook St.
1901, they are at 3 Fountain Cottage, Higher Crabtree, Michael, 75 yrs, stonebreaker, wife, Mary J, Lily, daughter, 14 yrs, Joseph 12 yrs, Ernest 9 yrs, John Wm Henry Bowden, stepson, 27 yrs, PO. in Royal Navy, b Okehampton. John Bowden is found in 1891 census RG12/1743,a boy, aged 16 yrs, Royal Navy, Devon at Impregnable.
