Narrative

Anna Elizabeth Warner, the wife of William was a patient woman who stood 4' 11" and weighed only 80 lbs. She loved music and sang alto in the quartet at the First Presbyterian Church in Duluth. She also enjoyed utilizing her special talent of painting dinner plates and wood burning designs into small wooden chests and music boxes.
Anna was a sensitive person and liked to quote the following: " Do all the good you can, in all the ways you can, to as many people as you can, and just as long as you can." " Look for the good in everyone and in everything." " One finds much joy in service to others, if done with a loving heart and a willing mind."
