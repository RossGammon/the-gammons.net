Narrative

On grave stone: "In Memory of Mrs Henry Cutcliffe died October 28, 1902 AET 65, How sweet to lay our sainted dead in such a place to sleep." Death Notice taken from "The Guardiam" 1902. "The death occured October 20, 1902 (assumed to be false date) in her 63 year at the residence of her son-in -law, Albert Murphy, Douglas St. of Bridget (Traynor) Cutcliffe, widow of Henry of Appin Road. She leaves 4 sons and 4 daughters besides a large circle of friends who mourn her depature from tis life. She was an industrous woman an ameable friend and a true Christian."
