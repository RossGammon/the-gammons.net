Narrative

Birth Certificate: Father Thomas Cutcliffe, woolcomber at Meddra St., Bideford. Mother Mary nee Popham. Marriage Certificate: Both of full age. He is a bachelor, a seamam residing at Pomphlett, his father Thomas Cutcliffe, a Woolcomber. She a spinster, also at Pomphlett, her father, John Sargeant, a Quarryman. In the presence of John Sargeant and Emma Hawkes. Census 1851: Pilton, Pilton St.: With parents Thomas and Mary. William age 6, Scholar, born Bideford. Census 1891: St. Andrews, Plymouth: Parmaster's House, Millbay Pier. William Cutcliffe, head age 47, Parmaster, born Bideford. Wife Mary J. Cutcliffe age 47, born Pomphlett. Son Ernest, age 16. Office Boy. Born Plymouth. Son Sidney, age 15, Errand Boy. Bortn Plymouth. Daughter Blanch age 12. Daughter Minnie, age 11, born Plymouth. Daughter Eveline, age 5, born Plymouth. Daughter Norah, age 4, born Plymouth.
