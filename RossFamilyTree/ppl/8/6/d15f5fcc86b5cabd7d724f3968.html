Narrative

Reginald was a school teacher and Mayor of Orange, a town of 30,000 people, 160 miles west of Sydney, Australia. Reginald was actively involved in human rights and social justice for the whole of his life. He was affectionately refered to as the Left Wing of the family as most of his family had been involved in farming wheat and raising cattle. Death caused by skin cancer.
