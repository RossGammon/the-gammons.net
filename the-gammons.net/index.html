Visiting the area of the birthplace of my Great Grandmother in Mount Gambier, South Australia.

Welcome to Ross Gammon’s Family History Website.

I have been researching my family tree since late 2004. For reasons of privacy and security, I will not be publishing details of any relatives that are still alive.

My hope is that members of my family will be able to refer to the website to see where the research has got to. My weblog (blog) can be used to check for a summary of the latest updates to the site. I also hope that the blog can be used as a mechanism for other family members to contribute to the research.

I am also conducting a One Name Study on the Gammon surname. A broad outline of the study can be found here.
